The year is 2121. Earth was destroyed by an asteroid last year which brings us to your current objective.

Your mission, should you choose to accept it, is to protect humanities last remaining planet from a similar fate.

You need to blast astroids away before they touch the beautiful planet which will take damage depending on the size of the astroid. If the health of the planet reaches 0%, the planet will explode and all humanity will be lost.

This job used to be easy but the speed of the asteroids seems to be ever increasing toward the seemingly doomed planet...
